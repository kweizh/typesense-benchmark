### `start.sh`

- Bulk-imports all 10 hubs (h01–h10) via the `/import` JSONL endpoint.

### `search.py`
- Accepts `--polygon "lat1,lng1,lat2,lng2,..."` and optional `--exclude-status <value>`.
- Builds Typesense's native polygon filter: `location:(lat1,lng1,lat2,lng2,...)` — a true point-in-polygon test, not a radius approximation.
- Combines with `status:!=<value>` when `--exclude-status` is given.
- Prints a single JSON line: `{"hub_ids": [...]}` with ids sorted ascending lexicographically.
